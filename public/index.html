  1. Welcome

    Tomatar is a Pomodoro Technique tool to help you stay productive to the max.

  2. The timer

    Hit the big button to start a pomodoro session. The time will wind down. You can hit it again to pause, in case you get interrupted.

  3. See what you've accomplished

    All of your pomodori are saved so you can track your progress.
